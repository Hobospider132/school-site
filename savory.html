    Egg Sandwich

    Follow along the video:

    Ingredients for mayonaise

    • 1 Egg
    • 2 Garlic
    • 1 Tsp sugar
    • 1/2 Tsp salt
    • Black pepper powder 1/2 Tsp
    • Yellow mustard 1/2 Tsp
    • 200 ml Oil
    • Milk powder 1 Tsp
    • Lemon juice 1 Tsp

    Ingredients for sandwich

    • 3 Hard boiled egg
    • 1 Tbsp mayonaise
    • 1 Tsp salt
    • 1 Tsp black pepper powder
    • Lemon juice 1 Tsp
    • Two pieces bread

    Steps for creating mayonaise

    1. Put one raw egg into the blender
    2. Put two finely chopped garlics into the blender
    3. Add 1 Tsp sugar to the mix
    4. Add 1/2 Tsp salt
    5. Add 1/2 Tsp black pepper powder
    6. Add 1/2 Tsp yellow mustard
    7. Blend for one minute
    8. Add 67 ml oil
    9. Add 1 Tbsp milk powder
    10. Add 67 ml oil again
    11. Blend for one minute
    12. Add 1 Tsp lemon juice
    13. Add 67 ml oil
    14. Blend for one minute
    15. Store in fridge for one week

    Steps for creating sandwich

    1. Remove the yolks from 3 hard boiled eggs
    2. Chop egg white finely
    3. Smush the egg yolks with the back of a fork
    4. Add 1 Tbsp mayonaise
    5. Add 1 Tsp black pepper powder
    6. Add 1 Tsp salt
    7. Mix well
    8. Add 1 Tbsp mayonaise
    9. Add egg whites
    10. Mix again
    11. Add 1 Tsp lemon juice
    12. Mix
    13. Spread onto some bread and place top piece of bread on it
