Chiffon cake

Follow along the video:

Ingredients for cake

  • 4 Egg yolks
  • 20g caster sugar
  • 60ml coconut oil
  • 70ml cocnut milk
  • 100g cake flour
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice
  • 80g caster sugar
  • 4 Egg whites

Baking the cake

  1. Crack the eggs pouring only the whites into a bowl
  2. Pour the egg yolks into a bowl and add 20g caster sugar
  3. Whisk thoroughly before adding 60ml coconut oil
  4. Whisk thoroughly before adding 70ml coconut milk
  5. Add 100g of cake flour before whisking thoroughly
  6. Whisk the egg whites
  7. Add 80g of caster sugar while continuing to whisk
  8. Add 1/2 tsp of lemon juice before whisking again
  9. Whisk until firm peaks
  10. Add to original egg yolk mix
  11. Mix together
  12. Pour into a pan with a diameter of 20 cm
  13. Bake at 170Â°C for 40-45 minutes
  14. Place pan upside down to cool
